Trinity University

Trinity University is located in San Antonio, Texas. It is the home to a select number of lucky students from all around the globe. Trinity is a liberal arts that provides its students with a well rounded education that will prepare them in all aspects of their future.


Photo courtesy of Trinity University

Trinity's campus is a beautiful one, inspired by an Italian village which can be seen in many aspects of its architecture. Size-wise trinity is very small which adds to the accessibility, homeness, and charm of Trinity University.

The features about Trinity that I appreciate most are the campus's attention to aesthetics in its' architecture, the close relationships forged between students and professors, and the rigor of the course that adequately prepares students.


I chose Trinity University because the moment I walked through the campus I was blown away and felt right at home. In all my college visits I liked the campuses but I was not excited about going to college until I thought about going to trinity.

I hope to major in art because it is my passion and I can think of no other path that would make me as happy as an art major would. I am also considering doubling my art major with a major in communication, making myself more employable and opening the world of advertising.
